Bagian 1: Metode Perhitungan FIFA Rankings

The FIFA rankings are calculated using a complex algorithm that takes into account a team's performance in international matches over the past four years. The algorithm is based on the following factors:

  • Match results: A team's win-loss ratio is the most important factor in determining its ranking.
  • Match importance: The ranking also takes into account the importance of the matches, such as the stage of the competition and the opponent's ranking.
  • Match performance: The performance of a team in its matches is also evaluated, including factors such as goals scored and conceded, yellow and red cards given, and disciplinary decisions.
