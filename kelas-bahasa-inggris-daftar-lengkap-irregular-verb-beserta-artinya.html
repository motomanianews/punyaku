Kelas Bahasa Inggris Daftar Lengkap Irregular Verb Beserta Artinya - Apakah Anda mencari daftar lengkap irregular verb beserta artinya untuk meningkatkan kemampuan bahasa Inggris Anda? Artikel ini menyajikan daftar lengkap irregular verb yang sering digunakan dalam bahasa Inggris, beserta artinya dan contoh kalimat.

Apa Itu Irregular Verb?

Irregular verb adalah kata kerja yang tidak memiliki aturan khusus dalam berubah bentuk, baik untuk dibentuk masa depan maupun masa lalu. Kata kerja ini memiliki bentuk yang tidak teratur dan harus diingat secara manual. Irregular verb sangat penting digunakan dalam komunikasi harian dan dalam berbagai situasi sosial.

Daftar Lengkap Irregular Verb Beserta Artinya

Berikut adalah daftar lengkap irregular verb beserta artinya:

Verb Starting with A

  1. Awake - to become awake
  2. Arrive - to come to a place
  3. Arts - to create something artistic

Verb Starting with B

  1. Be - to exist
  2. Become - to change into something
  3. Bite - to take a small amount of food

Verb Starting with C

  1. Catch - to take hold of something
  2. Change - to make something different
  3. Come - to move towards a place

Verb Starting with D

  1. Do - to perform an action
  2. Drive - to operate a vehicle
  3. Dwell - to live in a place

Verb Starting with E

  1. Eat - to take food into the body
  2. Escape - to get free from a situation
  3. Exist - to be in a condition

Verb Starting with F

  1. Feel - to have a sensation
  2. Find - to discover something
  3. Forget - to forget something

Verb Starting with G

  1. Go - to move from one place to another
  2. Give - to give something to someone
  3. Grow - to become larger

Verb Starting with H

  1. Have - to possess something
  2. Hear - to perceive sound
  3. Hide - to conceal something

Verb Starting with I

  1. Is - to exist in a certain state
  2. It - to refer to a thing
  3. Imitate - to copy someone or something

Verb Starting with J

  1. Jog - to run quickly
  2. Judge - to decide something
  3. Just - to be in a certain state

Verb Starting with K

  1. Keep - to retain something
  2. Know - to have knowledge
  3. Kill - to cause the death of someone or something

Verb Starting with L

  1. Learn - to acquire knowledge
  2. Leave - to go away from a place
  3. Live - to exist in a particular place

Verb Starting with M

  1. Make - to create something
  2. Manage - to control something
  3. Mention - to refer to something

Verb Starting with N

  1. Need - to require something
  2. Neglect - to forget or ignore something
  3. Noise - to make a loud sound

Verb Starting with O

  1. Off - to move away from something
  2. Often - to happen frequently
  3. Open - to uncover or reveal something

Verb Starting with P

  1. Pass - to move through a place
  2. Pay - to give something in exchange for something
  3. Provide - to make available something

Verb Starting with Q

  1. Quit - to stop doing something
  2. Quiet - to make a low sound
  3. Quote - to repeat someone's words

Verb Starting with R

  1. Read - to perceive written language
  2. Receive - to get something
  3. Reflect - to think about something

Verb Starting with S

  3. Sell - to give something in exchange for money

Verb Starting with T

  3. Think - to have a thought

Verb Starting with U
