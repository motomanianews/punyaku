The Arrowverse

The Arrowverse is a collection of TV shows that are connected by their shared theme of superheroes and DC Comics characters. The shows are:

  • Arrow (2012-2020)
  • The Flash (2014-present)
  • Legends of Tomorrow (2016-present)
  • Supergirl (2015-2021)
  • Batwoman (2019-present)

The Arrowverse is known for its gritty realism, complex characters, and epic storylines, which pay homage to the DC Comics universe. Each show has its own unique tone and style, but together, they form a cohesive and thrilling universe that explores the world of superheroes.

These shows may not be part of the Arrowverse, but they are all set within the DC Comics universe and offer unique twists and takes on beloved characters and storylines.
